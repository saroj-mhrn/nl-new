How It Works

  • Select Your Meals

    Pick the right meals for your child’s age and dietary preferences (even picky eaters!)

  • We Cook & Deliver

    Pediatric dietitian-approved meals shipped cold directly to your door.

  • Heat & Eat

    When mealtime arrives, just reheat in your microwave or oven for your kids to enjoy!
